Introduction

I lead client services and revenue growth for a Hi-Tech consulting portfolio, with primary responsibility for post-sale lifecycle management — onboarding, expansion, and retention across a set of enterprise technology accounts. This playbook is built around that context.

Most CRO frameworks are written for SaaS businesses — recurring product revenue, seat-based expansion, and product usage as the primary health signal. That model doesn't translate cleanly to consulting and AI services, where revenue is driven by relationships, delivery quality, and the ability to identify and close new workstreams within existing accounts. The metrics, the compensation structure, the onboarding process, and the health scoring model all need to reflect that reality.

This playbook is organised into eight sections: a live CRO dashboard, a revenue operating rhythm, a lead routing process, a sales compensation plan, a sales process, a customer success onboarding checklist, a customer health scoring model, and a preferred tech stack. Each section is tailored to a consulting revenue model — not adapted from a SaaS template. The goal is a playbook I can use in executive conversations, apply to current decisions, and evolve as the business grows.
