What's different from the SaaS class example

Content download is lower intent than a demo request, so the scoring step is critical before sales touches the lead. The flow adds an existing-customer branch — in a services model, expansion within accounts is dominant, and inbound activity from a customer's domain routes to the Account Lead, not a BDR. Roles are mapped to the services context: BD Lead handles new logos, Account Lead handles expansion, and the BDR sits between scoring and qualification with a tighter SLA for Tier-1 named accounts.
